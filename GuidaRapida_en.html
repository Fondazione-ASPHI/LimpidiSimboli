Introduction: Getting Started is Easy!

Welcome to the world of Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)! "Limpidi Simboli" is a free web application, created by Fondazione ASPHI Onlus, to translate words into symbols, facilitating communication for people with cognitive disabilities or language difficulties.

This guide will show you how to create and customize your first symbolic sentence in less than five minutes, with no complicated setup required. Are you ready? Let's start!

1. Create Your First Sentence in Symbols

  1. Write a Sentence: Enter a simple sentence in the large text field you see at the top.
  2. Translate!: Click the "üó£Ô∏è Translate" button (or just press Enter on your keyboard). You will immediately see your sentence transformed into a sequence of pictographic symbols from the ARASAAC library.
  3. Want to hear how the sentence sounds? Click the "üîä Listen" button to have the original sentence read aloud. This feature is very useful for reinforcing the connection between text, symbol, and sound, supporting learning and auditory comprehension.

Great! You've just created your first sentence in symbols. Now let's see how you can modify it to make it perfect for your needs.

2. Modify and Refine the Sentence

Sometimes the first symbol isn't the right one, or you want to make small adjustments. Here's how to customize your sentence quickly and easily.

2.1 Change a Symbol

If a symbol doesn't best represent the concept you want, the application offers alternatives.

  • Click repeatedly on the symbol you want to change to browse the available options.
  • Each click will show you a different option, if available.

    • You can upload high-resolution images
    • Images remain even after closing the browser

This feature is perfect for representing complex ideas that require multiple words. By merging multiple symbols, you can create a single pictogram that expresses the complete concept.
